# 编号

$$
x^+y^2=z^2 \tag{1}
$$

$$
x^+y^2=z^2 \tag{1$'$}
$$

$$
x^+y^2=z^2 \tag{1-1}
$$

$$
x^+y^2=z^2 \tag{*}
$$

$$
x^+y^2=z^2 \tag*{*}
$$
